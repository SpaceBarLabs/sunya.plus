Our Mission

Democratize access to the universe of open-source tools.

Unlocking the Power of Open Source

The world of open-source software is a vast universe of powerful tools. From managing your own news feeds to creating a private library of articles, there’s open-source solution for nearly everything. These tools give you the power to control your data and customize your digital environment. However, this power often comes with a steep learning curve, requiring technical expertise, server management, and constant maintenance.

Your Hosted Solution

We believe everyone should have access to these tools, not just those with the time and skills to manage a server. Our mission is to bridge that gap. We take the best open-source applications, handle all the complex setup and maintenance, and provide them to you as simple, affordable, and reliable services. You get all the benefits of powerful, community-driven software with none of the hassle.

A Commitment to the Community

We are not just users of open source; we are participants in its ecosystem. {{ site.name }} is built by Space Bar Labs on the fantastic work of projects like FreshRSS and Wallabag. To honor their contribution and to help ensure their continued development, we are committed to giving back. We contribute 10% of our profits directly to the open-source projects that power our services. When you subscribe to a Pleiades product, you're not just getting a great tool—you're supporting the community that made it possible.
